Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download InstaDownload?

You can download our iOS App from the App Store. We are eventually going to develop an Android version.

How can I download images and videos?

  • Open Instagram, find the image or video you want to download
  • Click on the ... button and select Copy Link
  • Come back to InstaDownload, paste the link and click Preview
  • Now you can download the image or video you selected!

The same works for videos?

Yes, same works for videos.

There is no 'Copy Link' button available on the image I'm trying to download?

That image or video belongs to a private account. We are only supporting publicly shared images and videos.

What are coins?

Coins are a means to get downloads. You use a coin to download an image or video.

How can I get coins?

You can go to the store section on the app and watch an ad to get coins.

How can I get unlimited downloads?

You can go to the store section and subscribe to our unlimited downloads service.
